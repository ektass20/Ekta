Story behind Noma



Noma is a vibrant, modern Mediterranean restaurant founded by Chef Danny Elmaleh, rooted in his Moroccan-Israeli heritage and a deep love for bold, honest food. The name “Noma” means “eatery” in Hebrew—simple, casual, and full of soul—just like our approach to cooking.
We opened our first location in Sherman Oaks, where a neighborhood vibe met sizzling skewers and crave-worthy falafel.
We’re proud to serve Los Angeles and keep the Mediterranean spirit alive—one delicious plate at a time. Come hungry, leave glowing..
