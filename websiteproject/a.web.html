Story Behind Mizlala

Mizlala is a vibrant, modern Mediterranean restaurant founded by Chef Danny Elmaleh, rooted in his Moroccan-Israeli heritage and a deep love for bold, honest food.

The name “Mizlala” means “eatery” in Hebrew—simple, casual, and full of soul.

We opened in Sherman Oaks, where a neighborhood vibe met sizzling skewers and crave-worthy falafel. We’re proud to serve Los Angeles and keep the Mediterranean spirit alive—one delicious plate at a time.
